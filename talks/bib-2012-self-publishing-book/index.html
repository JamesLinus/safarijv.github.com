Some assertions about authoring systems:

Domain-specific tools are going away.

What I think we’re coming to is the fact that moving from written (paper) typing on a typewriter to using any of the word-processing tools (that we've had in the last 20 years) all fundamentally have the same user interface. You are given a white rectangle to work with and you fill it up with the written word.

However, there are things that are happening outside the publishing industry. That’s causing my shift in thinking about whether that at least the white rectangle is the only form of input. Could we have two writing systems?

This isn’t really writing-specific but I noticed my own behavior shifting when it comes to GPS.

I used to buy a physical device that I put in my car that I would type in where I wanted to go and that would give me driving directions.
